Covid-19

Definition

Coronavirus is the most talked about topic of the present time .Corona virus which is commonly called Covid - 19 is an infectious disease. It causes illness in the respiratory system of human. It is a new virus that is impacting the whole world badly. It is spreading through contact from person to person.

History

Covid - 19 was firstly identified in December 2019 in Wuhan the city of China. In March 2020 WHO declared the covid - 19 outbreak the pendemic. Now it has spread throughout the world. The virus is spread by the droplets generated from the cough or sneeze of an infected person.

Symptoms /physical effects

Covid-19 symptoms can be very mild to severe. Some people have no symptoms. The most common symptoms are fever, dry cough and breathing problem. Besides these symptoms like fatigue, sore throat, muscle pain, loss of taste and smell can also be seen in the patients of Covid - 19

RECOMMENDATION

As we know no proper drug or Vaccine has been developed for the treatment of this disease yet. Prevention is the only cure of it. There are some that we can do such as

A.  Cover your nose and mouth with mask.

B.  Wash hands regularly with soap and sanitizer.

C.  Maintain a distance of at least 5 to 6 feet.

D.  Avoid touching your eyes, nose and mouth.

E.  Take such things which boost your immunsystem.

Negative Effects / Economical Effects

Several reports are showing that the number of Coronavirus infected countries is now almost 115 countries and territories.

The government declared lockdown to break the chain of covid - 19.

People had to face a lot of troubles due to it.

The labourers who earn, troubles and eat daily were out of food. They don't have any work because all the economic, social and educational activities were closed for a long time. It has affected our education badly. People lost their jobs, trades and business. Corona virus is one of the most severe issues that are being faced by the people around the world.
